A brand new second season!

iShows 2 brings a lot of new features like enhanced sorting, Discover mode, Trakt.tv integration and a new world of possibilities about customization.

And it looks beautiful on your iPad!

Don't search... Just Find!

iShows 2 introduces Discover, a new way to find TV shows.

You can browse through Trending series, Popular ones or see our own curated pick selection.

If you want to narrow down your list, you can use the genre filters in both Trending and Popular TV shows.

And for old schoolers, you can always use search.
By the way, you can make a long tap gesture in the add icon to go directly to the search field.

Trakt.tv integration

Using a Trakt.tv account, all your devices will be synced with your episodes up-to-date information.

You will also get recommendations based on your watched history and even know what TV shows your friends are following.

Learn more about how Sync works

An incredible level of customization

You can change the UI choosing between light and dark (perfect when watching TV shows at night) themes and more than 5 hilite colors.

Also, you can change between poster or fanart layouts, and choose the cover you like most in both layouts.
And don't forget to try the new condensed layout.

Finally, if you want to go a step beyond, change the whole iShows UI using one of the themes based on your favorite TV shows, like Kingslayer (Game of Thrones), Terminus (The Walking Dead) or Alburquerque (Breaking Bad).

Don't miss this new season!
